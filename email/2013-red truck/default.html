What does it take to become a Truck Driver?

Three Steps to Become a Professional Truck Driver

    1. Training. 3-4 weeks (160 hours) of CDL Class A Training. Possible job offers while you are in training.
    2. Orientation. Apply to different trucking companies. At Roadmaster you have choices where you would like to go work!
    3. Pass CDL Test. After passing your Roadmaster training and your CDL exam, you will be prepared as a fully qualified truck driver!
